Why to use Ralie Network?

Ralie Network is a community-driven MOOC provider and freelancer platform, where users are highly rewarded for using crypto to settle payments. Our mission is to help crypto become a mainstream form of payment for content and services, creating a user-friendly platform to attract tech-savvy crypto enthusiasts as well as mainstream users.

Ralie Network Features

Ralie Flagships are Ralie Learn and Ralie Work. To help foster our community and reward early adopters, a vast number of incentives will be offered during our first year of operations.

Ralie Learn

A Massive Open Online Course (MOOC) platform where users can buy video-classes on demand.

RIE Tokens are used to pay for content. An incentive program will reward outstanding teachers and best content creators on the platform.

Ralie Work

RIE Tokens are used to pay for services. Ab incentive program will reward freelancers and clients that successfully hire and get hired.

Why is Ralie Network different from the other projects?

One of the financial primitives that have been in evidence this past year is the Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAO). DAO changes the concept of a company having shareholders and allowing any HODLer to stake their tokens in a governance protocol and be some sort of "Outstanding Share Holder". It means that anyone can be part of the company's decision-making process and be entitled to a portion of its profits (dividends).

Which blockchain will Ralie Network use?

The RIE Token is an ERC20 token and will be deployed on the Ethereum Network.

The transaction fees on Ethereum won't be a problem?

Yes. But we are optimists about ETH2.0. We believe ETH2.0 PoS consensus mechanisms will allow us to develop a healthy ecosystem and an excellent user experience.

Does Ralie Network consider a different blockchain?

We are considering taking our platform to Solana. We are participating in the Solana x Serum DeFi Hackathon taking place this February (2021).

Does Ralie Network plan to have an ICO, IEO, or IDO?

We are planning to have an IDO, most probably on Polkastarter.com

Will the token be listed on the leading DEXes?

Most certainly, yes!

How many RIE tokens will be issued?

On deployment, 2.5 billion RIE Tokens will be created. From these 2.5 billion, 1 billion RIE Tokens will be locked in the PermaVault and unlocked monthly for ten (10) years.

What is RIE Tokens used for?

RIE tokens are used to settle payments on Ralie Network platforms, pay for fees, and stake in the governance protocol.

Ralie Network will only accept RIE Tokens as payment?

Initially yes. But in the long run, we plan to accept all cryptocurrency as long as both parties accept it. RIE Tokens will still be needed to pay for fees.

What is the target price for the RIE token?

We can not speculate on RIE token price. However, the initial pricing in Initial DEX Offering is U$0,014.
