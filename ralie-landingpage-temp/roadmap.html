Q1 2021

Deployment of the RIE Token, PermaVault and VestingVault on the Ethereum Network. Period Dedicated to Community Building. Seed Financing Round. 1st Airdrop.

Q2 2021

Period dedicated to the team expansion and preparation for Ralie Learn BETA launch. Launch of the LP reward program. DEX listing. DAO protocol launch.

Q3 2021

Ralie Learn BETA launch. DAO voting platform launch. APLHA testing phase of Ralie Work platform.

Q4 2021

Kickstart of the Layer 2 solutions development (Escrow, Payment Channels, etc). Start of the Community Incentives program, grants to outstanding users and DeFi incentive program. Ralie Work BETA release.

Q1-Q2 2022

Platform Improvements. Addition to more perks to Governance members and introduction of the goRIE token (more info to come). Cross-chain support.
