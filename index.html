A New Way to Connect

Why use Ralie Network?

Ralie Network is a community-driven MOOC provider and freelancer platform where users are highly rewarded for using crypto to settle payments. Our objective is to help crypto become a mainstream form of payment for content and services for tech-savvy crypto enthusiasts as well as for new users.

Ralie Network Features

The Ralie Network consists of two flagship platforms: Ralie Learn and Ralie Work. In order to foster our community platforms and reward early adopters, a vast number of incentives will be offered during our first operational year.

Ralie Learn

A Massive Open Online Course (MOOC) platform where users can buy video-classes on demand.

RIE Tokens are used to pay for content. An incentive program will reward outstanding teachers and best content creators on the platform.

Ralie Work

A Freelancer Platform where users can use their crypto-assets to hire and attract talents.

RIE Tokens are used to pay for services. An incentive program will reward clients and freelancers that successfully hire and get hired.

Governance

Early DAO members will get rewarded for staking their RIE tokens.

Become part of the DAO community and get to vote on decisions about the future of the platform. Stakers will receive a portion of the transaction fees on the platform and will help decide the ecosystem’s tokenomics.

Liquidity Providers

Be a liquidity provider for RIE pairs at our partner DEX aggregators and get rewarded.

Help us bootstrap the platform's economic ecosystem and get rewarded for holding LP Tokens.

Careers

Join the Ralie team. Check out the available positions and help us build the network.

Talents willing to help build the Ralie Network will be awarded with RIE token grants.

Fostering the DeFi Ecosystem

We are extending the incentive program to promote new DeFi solutions, conditioned by DAO voting.

Any project wiling to use the Ralie Platform to promote education of DeFi or develop teams to build DeFi products/services will be awarded with bonus RIE tokens.

Why is Ralie Network different from other projects?

One of the financial primitives that has seen explosive growth and popularity this past year is the Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO). DAO provides an entire altered concept of an “Outstanding Share Holder” as it allows members to stake their tokens in a governance protocol. In summary, DAO grants stakers an ability to make decisions and receive a portion of transaction fees on the network.

Which blockchain will Ralie Network use?

The RIE Token is an ERC20 token and will be deployed on the Ethereum Network.

Will transactions fees on the Ethereum network be an initial problem?

In the short term, yes. However, we’re optimistic about the ETH 2.0 upgrade. ETH 2.0 PoS consensus mechanisms will help to develop a healthy ecosystem and an excellent user experience.

Is Ralie Network considering a different blockchain?

We are considering bringing the Ralie Network platform to the Solana blockchain. Ralie Network will participate in the Solana x Serum DeFi Hackathon taking place in February 2021.

Does Ralie Network plan on conducting an ICO, IEO, or IDO?

We plan on conducting an IDO most likely through Polkstarter.com

When will I receive RIE tokens from the 1st Airdrop?

RIE tokens from the first airdrop will be issued and sent out on March 17th.

Will the RIE token be listed on popular crypto exchanges?

While the RIE token will not immediately be listed on any exchange, we hope to see RIE tokens listed at some point in the future.

Will the token be listed on leading DEXes?

Yes!

How many RIE tokens will be minted?

Upon launch of the Ralie Network, 2.5 billion RIE tokens will be minted. From the minted amount, 1 billion RIE tokens will be locked in PermaVault, and the deposited token will gradually unlock monthly for a duration of 10 years.

What is the RIE token used for?

RIE tokens are used to settle payments on the Ralie Learn and Ralie Work platforms, pay for fees, and to stake in the governance protocol.

Will Ralie Network only accept RIE tokens as payment?

Initially yes. However, in the long run, we plan to consider all cryptocurrencies contingent that both parties mutually accept the use of the token. RIE tokens will still be needed to pay for transaction fees.
